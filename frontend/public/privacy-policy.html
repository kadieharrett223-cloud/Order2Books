Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

Order2Books ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and used when you install and use the Order2Books app.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect limited information necessary to operate the service, including Shopify store domain, order information required for synchronization, QuickBooks invoice/customer information required for syncing, and app configuration settings.

2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used only to synchronize orders from Shopify to QuickBooks, generate invoices in QuickBooks, maintain connections, and provide technical support.

3. Data Storage

Data may be temporarily stored to process synchronization tasks between Shopify and QuickBooks. We do not sell or rent merchant data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with Shopify and QuickBooks Online. These services operate under their own terms and privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect information used by the App. However, no transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to operate synchronization services or comply with legal obligations.

7. User Responsibilities

Users remain responsible for reviewing accounting records in QuickBooks and Shopify to ensure accuracy. Changes made directly within QuickBooks may take up to 24 hours to be reflected in the App.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates are posted with a revised "Last Updated" date.
